Contributions

I was brought on during preproduction and contributed to the early prototypes, greyboxes and designs for all of the in games residential, commercial and industrial (RCI) assets. I was influential in delivering the distinctive Maxis style and prototyping the visions from art directors, production and tech art.

We had incredibly tight polygon budgets that required critical thinking to work effectively. The team relied on me to document technical art workflows and update the pipeline info to get new artists and outsourcing partners up to speed.

The 3d artist role in a similation game also required me code a lot of technical data for the in game units. Placement for actors, building lot objects, cars, dumpsters, signage, VFX, lights, rubble, walking paths, foliage, bounding boxes, ectâ€¦

In addition to modeling, UV layout, texturing, buildings, I had also animated a great deal of the buildings props and functions.

Additional technical work I had sucess with was through authoring 3 levels of LODs for each building asset. *This work was done by hand because our models were non manifold geo. The Simcity Build-it Team in Helsinki would later go in to use these models in their game too!

The Simcity project was a AAA title that I contributed greatly to.
